Fallout Boy

About the band

Fallout Boy is an American rock band formed in Wilmette, Illinois, a suburb of Chicago, in 2001. The band consists of lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist Patrick Stump, bassist Pete Wentz, lead guitarist Joe Trohman, and drummer Andy Hurley. The band originated from Chicago's hardcore punk scene, with which all members were involved at one point.


The band members include:

  1. Patrick Stump (vocals, guitar)
  2. Pete Wentz (bass guitar, backing vocals)
  3. Joe Trohman (guitar, backing vocals)
  4. Andy Hurley (drums)

Some of their most popular albums are:

  • Take This to Your Grave (2003)
  • From Under the Cork Tree (2005)
  • Infinity on High (2007)
